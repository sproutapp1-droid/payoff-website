# SEO Blog Content Plan

## Blog Infrastructure
- Posts are MDX files in `src/content/blog/`
- Frontmatter: title, description, date, author, category, tags
- Categories: Guides, Strategies, Features, Wellness, Planning
- Blog is English-only (not under [lang] routing)
- Schema markup auto-generated by `blog/[slug]/page.tsx`

## Content Guidelines
- 150-250 lines per post
- Warm, empathetic, emotionally supportive tone
- Include 2-3 CTAs per post (mid-article CTABox + closing CTA)
- Internal links to calculators and related blog posts
- Use rich MDX components (see blog-template.md)
